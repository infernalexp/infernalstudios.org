Mining Master

Available for 1.18 - 1.20.1

Aiming to further incentivise mining and spice up the process of earning Enchantments, Mining Master adds numerous 'Enchanting Gems' across all three dimension that bestow Enchantments when utilised in a Smithing Table, and which can even make whole new tools and armour in the brand new Gem Forge!

Food Effects

Available for 1.16 - 1.19.2

Encouraging players to break the Porkchop Meta, Food Effects gives each food item a unique effect upon consuming it! A powerful config allows you to do this for any food item from any mod, too!

Neko's Enchanted Books

Available for 1.18 - 1.20.4

Giving every Enchanted Book a unique textures without the need for Optifine, Neko's Enchanted Books is also widely compatible with numerous other popular mods too.

Celestial Config

Available for 1.18.2 - 1.21

Celestial Config lets you change the size of the sun and the moon!

Everything Descriptions

Available for 1.18 - 1.20.4

Want to add immersive in-game guidance without adding guide books? Everything Descriptions has your back!

Elyta Bounce

Available for 1.18 - 1.19.2

With a hold of the jump key, Elytra Bounce has you bump off the ground to continue flying! Pairs great with a mod that removes Elytra Rockets like Aileron.

Shield Expansion

Available for 1.18.2 - 1.20.1

Shield combat is made far more fun and interesting via Shield Expansion, which adds tiers, parrying, and cooldowns to enhance the combat experience to a great degree!
